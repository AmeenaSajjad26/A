Miss A Online Space

I Love Teaching!

Welcome to Ms. A's online class! I can't wait to take a journey of understanding letter sounds, word recognition, and comprehension with your scholars. Hats off to them for their successes and I love to celebrate it with them.

My name is Ms. A and I have been teaching for seven years now. I have a double degree in education. Success in literacy is our top priority and now that can be achieved during these unprecedented times, from the safety of your own home.

Rest assured that learning online can be fun _and_ interactive! I love to have them hop as a bunny while they spell the word hop. Their faces light up as they get to come alive on the screen. Take my hand and allow me to help you help your child grow in one of the most important subjects of their entire education.
